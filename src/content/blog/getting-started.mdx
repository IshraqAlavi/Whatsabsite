# Getting Started with WhatsAble

Welcome to WhatsAble! This guide will help you set up your first WhatsApp automation in just a few minutes.

## Choose Your Product

WhatsAble offers three powerful products for different needs:

### 1. Notifyer System
Our flagship enterprise platform for unlimited messaging from your own brand/number via WhatsApp Business API (WABA).

**Best for:** Businesses who need their own WhatsApp number with advanced features.

### 2. Notifier Bot
Simple automation via our verified bot number with quick setup.

**Best for:** Small businesses starting with WhatsApp automation.

### 3. WhatsAble Bot
Internal alerts and team notifications.

**Best for:** Sending messages to yourself or your team.

## Quick Setup

1. **Sign up** for your chosen product
2. **Connect** your favorite automation tool (Zapier, Make, n8n)
3. **Send** your first message
